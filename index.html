Ph.D. Candidate in Computer Science & Engineering
The Ohio State University

Research Interests

  • AI/ML for Precision Oncology & Computational Pathology
    • Developing computer vision algorithms and multi-modal fusion techniques (Histology & Spatial Omics) for cancer diagnosis and prognosis.
  • Foundation Models & Big Data
    • Leveraging large-scale public datasets to build robust, generalized representations for biomedical data through domain-specific fine-tuning.
  • Spatial Bioinformatics
    • Statistical modeling of Tumor heterogeneity and cell-cell interactions.
  • Privacy-Preserving Healthcare AI
    • Implementing secure learning frameworks to utilize sensitive medical data across institutions.
